Our Adoption Process

Adopting a pet is a big commitment, and we're here to help you through every step. Our process ensures that every animal goes to a loving, responsible home.

Choose Your Pet

Browse our available dog breeds and find the one that captures your heart. Visit our shelter to meet them in person and see how you connect.

Submit an Application

Fill out our adoption application. We'll ask about your lifestyle, experience with pets, and home environment to ensure a good match.

Home Visit & Interview

One of our adoption counselors will visit your home and discuss your pet care plans. This helps us ensure the best possible outcome for both you and your new pet.

Meet & Greet

If approved, you'll have a supervised meeting with your potential new family member. This is your chance to bond and see how you interact.

Adoption Finalization

Once everything looks good, you'll sign the adoption contract and take your new pet home! We provide all necessary paperwork and first-week supplies.

Follow-Up Support

We check in with you during the first few weeks and months to ensure a smooth transition. We're always here if you need advice or support.

Adoption Requirements

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Provide proof of a stable residence
  • Have the time and resources to care for a pet
  • Be willing to provide veterinary care
  • Have a suitable living environment for the pet
  • Be prepared for the long-term commitment (10-15+ years)

Adoption Fees

Our adoption fees help cover veterinary care, vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and microchipping. Fees vary by animal but typically range from $75-$200. This fee ensures every pet leaves our center healthy and ready for their new home.
