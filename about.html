Our Story

Pup Palace Adoption Center was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide loving homes for animals in need. What started as a small rescue operation has grown into a full-service adoption center serving our community and beyond.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Every animal deserves kindness and respect
  • Integrity: We match pets with families who will provide the best possible care
  • Community: We work with local shelters and volunteers to help as many animals as possible
  • Education: We help families understand pet care and responsibilities

Our Facilities

Our center features spacious play areas, comfortable kennels, and a full veterinary clinic. We ensure all our animals receive proper medical care, socialization, and plenty of love while they wait for their forever homes.
